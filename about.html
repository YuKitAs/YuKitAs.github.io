> My passion for photography is probably inherited from my father, who sent me my first SLR carema. In 2016 I decided to focus on wildlife photography, as I've always had an affinity for animals. It's been a tough challenge for me, requiring tons of patience, sensitive perception, high agility, professional equipments as well as knowledge of the behaviors and habits of wild animals. Being a poor beginner, I'll sometimes visit wildlife parks for practice, shooting photos of animals like raccoons, foxes and lynxes in the hope of greeting them one day in the real wild.

It's much less time-consuming. And what's more, you can send a hand-made pic to others while still keep the original copy for yourself.
